    GLEN RECRUITERS

    Glen Recruiters, Your HR Specialists

    Glen Recruiters is one of the most prominent staffing and recruitment companies in the Lower Mainland. We provide recruitment services for every industry, like Traffic Control, Security, Construction, Warehouse Staffing, and Management. Our dedicated and experienced recruitment team works with you to find the perfect candidate. At Glen, we ensure all potential candidates for you are pre-screened, interviewed, and then selected based on your needs. Hence, we assure you that the candidate we offer will become your star employee. Our candidates have a wide range of experience, from warehouse workers, security guards, forklift drivers, and office administrators to executives. We enjoy the challenges of being known as the "Career Shapers" for employees. We work with our employees throughout the interview and hiring processes by training, guiding and mentoring them.
